Description:

WorldTour periodically sets your home wallpaper to beautiful pictures of places from all around the world!

The images are fetched every 30 minutes from LIVE webcams so you can always see the current view of the location.
Explore the world, and enjoy the view!

Available webcams include:
- The Eiffel Tower
- Earth view from space
- New York City
- Penguins in Antarctica
- The Grand Canyon
...and more!

You can choose a specific webcam or select random mode to be surprised at every refresh.
A widget is also available if you prefer to keep your current wallpaper.
The refresh frequency can be adjusted in the settings.
You can also add your own webcams to the list.
